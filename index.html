NATO Phonetic Alphabet Converter

This tool converts letters and numbers into the NATO phonetic alphabet, which is used to clearly communicate spelling over radio, phone, or in any situation where clarity is crucial.

For example, "BAT" becomes "Bravo Alpha Tango". This helps avoid confusion between similar-sounding letters like 'B' and 'P' or 'M' and 'N'.
